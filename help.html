Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place a bid?

To place a bid, navigate to the auction details page, enter your bid amount, and click the "Place Bid" button. Your bid will be processed in real-time.

How do I register as a seller?

Simply visit our registration page, select the seller option, and fill out the required details. An approval process will follow to ensure your account meets our seller criteria.

What payment methods are accepted?

We support various payment methods including credit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers. Please refer to our payment policy for detailed information.
